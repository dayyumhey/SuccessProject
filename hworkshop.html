Wired and Wireless Robotics (Basic-LEVEL 1)

Duration:- 2 Days (10 Hours).
Wired and Wireless Robotics workshop is the first (a) gateway to enter in the world of robotics. This workshop deals with all the basic concepts of robotics, movements, communication, selection of components/material according to the requirement, etc. In this workshop participants will make a wired robot and then modify it to a wireless robot.

Workshop Topics:

  • What is a Robot?
  • Types of Robot.
  • Basic materials in making a robot.
  • Types of materials and criteria for selecting materials.
  • Universal movement of robots.
  • Making of wired robot.
  • Understanding wireless communication.
  • Implementing wireless communication and testing it.
  • Making of wireless robot.

 

Skills Enhanced:

Participant will be able to make any type of wired and RF based wireless robot.

Line Follower Robotics (Intermediate-LEVEL 3)

Duration:- 2 Days (10 Hours).
A Line Follower Robot is an automated guided vehicle, which follow a visual line embedded on the floor usually, the visual line is the path in which the line follower robot goes and it will be a black line on a white surface but the other way.For making line follower Arduino will be used to build the logic. Line follower is the first step towards automation.

Workshop Topics:

  • Basic programming of Arduino
  • Understanding line follower sensor
  • Interface line follower sensor
  • Reading black and white lines
  • Logic for movement of robot in Arduino

 

Skills Enhanced:

  • Participant will be able to able to program Arduino and design their ownRobot with automated movements.
